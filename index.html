Privacy Policy

Welcome to Al-Hikmah. Your privacy is of utmost importance to us.

  • No Data Collection: We do not store, track, or sell your personal information.
  • Location Services: The city and country you enter are strictly used to fetch real-time Prayer and Azan timings from public APIs (AlAdhan). This data is processed locally on your device.
  • Permissions: The auto-azan feature requires basic audio playback permissions.
  • Content: All Quranic text, audio, and Hadiths are sourced from authentic public APIs (AlQuran.cloud, EveryAyah, and Open Hadith).
